About

From the storied workrooms of Huntsman, Finn's craft has been shaped by the highest standards of both Saville Row heritage and modern design. Mentored by some of Saville Row's most respected artisans, he honed his skill through years of dedication to precision, fix, and form. Finn is redifining bespoke tailoring for a new generation - bridging timeless craftsmanship with a distinctly contemporary eye.

Finn is a Saville Row trained fine tailoring specialist, with 10 years of experience. He leads fittings and client relationships and has developed skills in salesmanship, team leadership, training, alteration and production management. He is currently based in New York.
